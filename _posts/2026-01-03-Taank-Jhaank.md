---
title: "Taank/Jhaank"
date: 2024-12-20
categories: Kalaam
tags: Urdu
---


Kabhi bewajah ka koi kaam ho  
Ya bematlab ki koi baat ho  
Ishaaro mein Chai coffee ka nyota ho  
Ya faqat sirf tehelna he ho  
Har baar mere andar ka chor dar jaata hai  
Ki is baar to tum samajh he jaogi  
Ki ye sab bahaane hain tumhari raqbat ke  
Tumhare paas rehne ke, tumhe paas rakhne ke  

Main jab bhi koi naya shagufa chhodta hoon  
Ya kisi khatam hoti baat ko aur kheenchta hoon  
Tehela tehla kar behla behla kar sunta sunata hoon  
Tumhari ek baat par josh mein chaar keh jaata hoon  
Tumhari aankho ki harkat aur hothon ki jumbish dekhta hoon  
Aur sochta hoon ki shayad tum is baar samajhogi  
ki Baat wo nahi hai jo main keh raha hoon,  
Asal baat wo hai jo main keh nahi paa raha hoon  

Tumhare paas hone par Darr jaata hoon ki kahin  
Haath badha kar tumhe sach mein chhoo na loon  
Mere chhoone se ye khwab kahin bikhar na jaaye  
Aur phir kho jaata hoon honi-anhoni ke vasvason mein
