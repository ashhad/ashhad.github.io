---
title: "Azaad Insan"
date: 2025-07-05
categories: Kalaam
tags: Urdu
---

Agar socha jaaye to  
Aasan nahi hai insaan hona  
Nadi ke paas sahooloiyat hai  
Ek baarish se agar bhar jaaye  
To ufna kar behne lagti hai  
Aadmi ke paas ye ijazat nahi  
Seene tak bhar aane par bhi  
Ek ek qatra hisab se bahata hai  
Khule aasman aur zameen ke beech  
Jo ped saalon se khade hain  
Wo kabhi ghutan se nahi marte  
Par usi hawa aur paani mein  
Ummeedon ki ghutan se  
Aadmi roz mar mar ke jeeta hai  
Kabhi kisi parinde ki parwaz  
Hasraton ke bojh se nahi dabi  
Puraane se puraane ped ke tane mein  
Mare hue khwabo ki qabr-gaah nahi milti
